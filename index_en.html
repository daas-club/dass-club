About DAAS

A student club at King Saud University, that interested in the field of drones and autonomous systems.

Vision

An empowered student work environment to implement and support innovative practical projects in the field of drones and autonomous systems.

Message

Building capabilities and enabling innovation and creativity in the field of drones and autonomous systems to achieve the objectives of the Kingdom’s Vision 2030.

President Speech

Building capabilities and enabling innovation and creativity in the field of drones and autonomous systems to achieve the objectives of the Kingdom’s Vision 2030.

Objectives

Discovering talents and nurturing talented people in the field of drones and autonomous systems.

Participation in various events internally and externally to meet with specialists and exchange experiences.

Creating the appropriate environment for students to develop their abilities and skills, exchanging experiences among them, and working to encourage, support and honor them.

Documenting student production and creating a database of student talents.

Providing intensive courses for students to contribute to supporting and implementing their creative ideas.

Providing job opportunities and training programs from specialized and supportive entities.

Organizing meetings and dialogue sessions with experts and specialists in the field of drones and autonomous systems.
